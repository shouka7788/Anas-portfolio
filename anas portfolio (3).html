Who I Am

About Me

"Language is the road map of a culture. It tells you where its people come from and where they are going."

I am a passionate English teacher with classroom experience in primary and middle school education. I specialize in designing engaging lessons, incorporating technology, and fostering a fun and supportive learning environment.

Beyond the classroom, I am a filmmaker, director, and graphic designer. I film, direct, and edit school TV-style programs, bringing creativity and storytelling into everything I do. I enjoy helping students grow in confidence and language fluency.
